About Us

At SRI SAI ITI, we are committed to providing quality vocational education that prepares students for successful careers in various fields. Our focus is on delivering hands-on training and skill development that empowers our students to excel in the workforce.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between industry needs and skill development, ensuring that our students are job-ready from day one.

We offer a wide range of courses in fields such as technology, engineering, and business management to help students pursue their passions and build a successful future.

Mission

To impart quality Industrial Training to make trainees skilled industrial workers to be quite eligible for industrial as well as civil society.

Vision

To maintain the tempo of quality training up with the technology upgradation.

Motto

WORK IS WORSHIP

Objectives

  • To meet the growing demand for skilled workers in the industries.
  • To make the trainees a responsible citizen of our society.
  • To produce technical manpower for our country.
  • To make the trainees eligible to be employed.
  • To enable the trainees to be self-employed.
