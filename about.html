About Us

At SRI SAI ITI, we are committed to providing quality vocational education that prepares students for successful careers in various fields. Our focus is on delivering hands-on training and skill development that empowers our students to excel in the workforce.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between industry needs and skill development, ensuring that our students are job-ready from day one.

We offer a wide range of courses in fields such as technology, engineering, and business management to help students pursue their passions and build a successful future.

Mission

To impart quality Industrial Training to make trainees skilled industrial workers to be quite eligible for industrial as well as civil society.

Vision

To maintain the tempo of quality training up with the technology upgradation.

Motto

WORK IS WORSHIP

Objectives

  • To meet the growing demand for skilled workers in the industries.
  • To make the trainees a responsible citizen of our society.
  • To produce technical manpower for our country.
  • To make the trainees eligible to be employed.
  • To enable the trainees to be self-employed.

Spacious Classrooms

Our classrooms are designed to provide a comfortable and conducive learning environment, equipped with modern teaching aids and ample space for interactive sessions.

Self Examination Centre

Our Self Examination Centre is equipped with the latest technology to facilitate effective learning and assessment. Students can take practice tests and receive instant feedback to improve their skills.

Library

Our library is a treasure trove of knowledge, offering a wide range of books and resources to support the learning journey of our students. It's a quiet space for study and research.

Play Area

Our play area is designed for relaxation and recreation, providing students with a space to unwind and engage in physical activities.

Diesel Mechanic Lab

Our Diesel Mechanic Lab is equipped with the latest tools and machinery, providing students with hands-on experience in diesel engine maintenance and repair.

Computer Lab

Our Computer Lab is equipped with modern computers and software, providing students with the necessary skills for the digital age. It's a space for practical learning and innovation.

Electrician/Electronics Lab

Our Electrician/Electronics Lab is designed to provide students with practical skills in electrical and electronic systems. It is equipped with modern tools and equipment for hands-on training.

Renewable Power Source

Our Renewable Power Sourced Institute focuses on sustainable energy solutions, providing students with knowledge and skills in renewable energy technologies.
